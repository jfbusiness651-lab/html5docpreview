Why choose ePAL?

Read this topic to get an overview of the business challenges and how ePAL can help you solve these.

Challenges

As an internatational selller with a WooCommerce store you may face the following challenges:
  • Tax calculation can be difficult for international sales. This is a multi-faceted problem that covers issues such as the following:
    • Indirect tax rate calculation. The applicable indirect tax rate can vary for different jurisidiction.
    • You also need to account for different tax rates within a tax rate. For example, some goods may qualify for a reduced tax rate or may be exempt from tax or zero rated.
    • Tax on shipping can be difficult to calculate and it is often estimated or overchaged.
    • Product classification can be challenging.
    • B2B sales also bring specific tax requirements.
    • Tax schemes that are designed to help importers reach international customers such as Import One-Stop Shop (IOSS) may have their own specific requirements.
  • Customs and excise duty can bring additional requirements when compared to selling domestically where only.
  • Once your goods clear customs, you need to ensure that they are shipped to the correct lcoation. This depends on accurate shipping labels and these may need to be coordinated over multiple shipping partners.
  • Product classification can be a time consuming and recurring chore.

Solutions

ePAL helps address these challenges by providing the following solutions:
  • VAT Calculation and Reporting: ePAL identifies the correct VAT rate (including reduced- and zero-rated goods) for the source and destination countrie, calculates the correct VAT for each product, and generates compliant reports for the remittance of VAT in each country.
  • Customs and Excise: ePAL automatically identifies product classification codes for the destination country, calculates the correct Customs Duties, checks for Excise products, and provides you with verifiable and audit-ready proof.
  • Shipping and Labelling: ePAL provides easy access to hundreds of shipping companies to give your customer the best options and rates, and generates shipping labels and documentation that is compliant with the Customs requirements for the destination Country.
  • Refunds and Returns: Easy international refunds and returns process, pre-printed labels and documentation, automatic calculation of VAT and Duties reclaims.
