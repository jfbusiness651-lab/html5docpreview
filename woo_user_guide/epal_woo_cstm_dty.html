Customs Duty and Excise

This topic explains how customs duties and excise affect the calculation of your fully landed cost.

Customs Duty

Customs duties are taxes levied on goods when they cross a border between countries or trade zones. For example, goods being imported into the European Union (EU) from Great Britain are subject to customs duty.

ePAL helps you to calculate these and contribute to the calculation of the fully landed costs.

Excise checker

Excise duty is a tax on specific goods like alcohol or cigarettes. These can affect the tax and duty that may apply.

ePAL will check goods to ensure that excisable goods are not part of the basket.
