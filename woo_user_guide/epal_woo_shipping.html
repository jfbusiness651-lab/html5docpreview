Shipping

This topic explains how ePAL integrates with the native WooCommerce shipping options.

ePAL provides the following fearures that are related to shipping:
  • Shipping Rates
  • Tax on Shipping
  • Address Validation
  • Shipping labels
  • Custom Shipping Address
Product classification is also an important part of shipping. You can read about how ePAL helps you with this in the Product Classification topic.

Shipping Rates

You can avail of shipping services from multiple providers such as DPD, UPS. FedEx, DHL and others. You can avail of express, standard, or economy options. Rates can also be cached for up to 30 minutes.

If you have an existing shipping provider, you can integrate their service with ePAL.

If you do not have a shipping provider, ePAL will return some options for you to choose from. This can be useful if you do not have a shipping provider for a particular country.

Address Validation

Many shipping service providers require specific, valdiated address information to faciliate their service. ePAL helps you by helping you provide the needed information and to validate that it is correct. This service ìs facilitated by integration with Hurricane.

Address information can be provided in the Custom Shipping Address setting and in the checkout form. The following components

We recommend that you add Postal Codes for all countries. It is required for Spain, the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland (UK), and Spain. You must provide it for these countries.

States or provinces are subdivisions within a country. The following countries require that you specify the state or subdivision in address information:
  • Australia
  • Brazil
  • Canada
  • India
  • UK
  • United States of America
